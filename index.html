First of all, let's understand the process! To use the site, you must click the Sign Up button to register, and then log in to the site using the Sign In button. After entering the site, select the "University for Master" button if you are a Master's student, and the "University for Bachelors" button if you are a Bachelor's student. , you can get the code prepared in advance for you, which is needed to learn your prices and daily processes. After receiving the code, you can go back to the Home page, click on "U n i v e r s i t i e s" , select the name of the university where you studied and add the relevant information. You can also save as much money as you want on this site and use that money whenever you want. At Welcome Your Account , you can also find brief information about your university, specialty, teachers and teammates. This site, developed under the auspices of the Ministry of Education of Azerbaijan, is always at your service. Let enough Azerbaijani students be worthy representatives of our clear future :) ...
